Submit Your Music

How to send your files?

To submit your project to the channel, please follow these simple steps:

  • Hosting: Use a reliable file-sharing service like MediaFire, WeTransfer, or Google Drive.
  • Required files:
    • Your music track (.mp3 or .wav format).
    • A thumbnail image (.jpg or .png format).
    • Your music video if you have one (.mp4 format).
  • Submission: Once your files are uploaded, copy the sharing link and send it to me at: submit.ytbaudio@yahoo.com.

Make sure the links are set to "public" or "anyone with the link" so I can download them!
